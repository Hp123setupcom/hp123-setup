How to connect a printer via a wireless network?

Visit 123.hp.com/laserjet for wired and wireless HP printer setup. Follow these steps to connect a wireless printer:

  • Turn on the printer and connect to your home WiFi.
  • Choose the network, manage Wireless LAN Settings, and locate the SSID.
  • Enter the password, open printer settings, and add your printer.
  • Visit 123.hp.com/setup to download and install drivers.
